## II. Why "project dynamics" is the right ask (and why it works)

This is the **classic Chinese-media PR cadence**: a single feature article establishes the narrative; rolling "project dynamics" announcements keep the project visible across the syndication network for weeks afterward.

In the CN-language crypto media ecosystem (Cointime, PANews, BlockBeats, TechFlow, 深潮, 巴比特, 链得得, 火星财经, 金色财经), "项目动态" (project dynamics) is a recognized content slot:

- 100–300 字 short pieces
- One angle per piece
- Visible on "项目动态" / "项目快讯" feeds
- Republished across networks rapidly
- Each piece reinforces the broader narrative without re-pitching it

**This is how Chinese-market crypto PR generates sustained presence** — not one big launch, but a steady cadence of short, focused updates that compound visibility.
