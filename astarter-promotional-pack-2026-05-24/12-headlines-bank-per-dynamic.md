## Headlines NOT to use (banned)

- Anything with "moon", "100x", "alpha", "gem", "must-buy"
- Anything that implies financial advice
- Anything that fabricates a number or quote
- Anything that names a competitor in a derogatory way
- Anything that promises a date for TGE / listing that we haven't formally announced
- Click-bait formulas ("You won't believe...", "The shocking truth about...")
