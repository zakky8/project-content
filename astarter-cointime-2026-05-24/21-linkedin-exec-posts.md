# LinkedIn Posts — Executive Voice Variants

Three voice variants written for personal LinkedIn distribution from named executives. Each adapts the thesis to a different professional audience and uses the conventions of that lane.

**Posting hygiene:**
- Always personal account, never company page (LinkedIn organic reach favors personal accounts ~10x)
- First 2 lines are the hook — LinkedIn truncates the rest
- 5–7 line breaks between paragraphs for mobile readability
- One hashtag block at end, max 5 hashtags
- Reply to every comment in the first 4 hours
