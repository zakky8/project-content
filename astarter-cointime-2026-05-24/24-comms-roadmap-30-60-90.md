# 30/60/90-Day Comms Roadmap

**Owner:** Zakky (with GK approval per milestone)
**Anchor:** Cointime feature publish date = Day 0
**Purpose:** Convert one announcement into a quarter of compounding momentum, not a single news cycle.

The discipline: every week ships at least one piece of net-new content, and every month closes at least one major proof point. Without continuous shipping, the May 15 announcement decays into noise.

## Days 30–60 — Proof-Point Wave

The shape of Days 30–60 depends entirely on which milestones land. Three scenarios:

### Scenario A — DeFAI execution layer beta ships ahead of schedule
- Launch announcement (new long-form + new flash, same playbook as Day 0)
- First external builder spotlight (case study format)
- Renewed press push to outlets that passed in Phase 1
- Open closed-beta for select partners

### Scenario B — Second strategic alliance announced
- Joint announcement repeating Cointime / PANews / BlockBeats playbook
- LinkedIn post pattern (Variants A/B/C) for second alliance
- Update visual system with second partner branding
- Hold a joint Spaces conversation

### Scenario C — No major milestone yet
- Shift to thought-leadership: weekly substack-style essays on agent settlement
- Recycle and remix Day 0 assets into video shorts (vertical PNG → 60s reels)
- Investor / partner conversations driven by `22-investor-faq.md` rather than press
- Quiet positioning, not loud announcing

**Day 60 checkpoint:** if no Scenario A or B has triggered, hold further press pushes until a real proof point lands. Continuing to broadcast without substance burns credibility.

## Continuous (every week, always-on)

- **Tuesday:** One narrative-anchored post on @AstarterDefiHub (thread or quote tweet of relevant industry news, tying back to the thesis)
- **Thursday:** One LinkedIn post from rotating exec voice
- **Friday:** Community update in Discord / Telegram
- **Anytime:** Respond to every press inquiry within 4 hours during business days

---

## What kills this roadmap (red flags to escalate)

- More than 14 days without a net-new piece of content shipping
- Press inquiries unanswered for >48 hours
- Community sentiment in Discord / TG turning sharply negative without response
- Any milestone slipping >2 weeks without internal communication first
- Competitor announcement that lands in our exact category and we don't respond within 72 hours

---

## Budget / capacity check

This roadmap assumes:
- 1 full-time comms lead (Zakky) + part-time exec time + part-time community
- Approx $0 paid media — entirely owned + earned
- All copy and visual assets sourced from this `project-content` repo + ad-hoc additions

If a paid media line opens, the natural augmentations: (a) Twitter promoted threads, (b) crypto-newsletter sponsorships (Milk Road, Bankless, The Defiant), (c) podcast underwriting. None recommended in Phase 1 — earn the audience before paying for it.
