## V. Holder-specific FAQ (lives in Discord pin + docs site)

### "Wait, are you saying my AA is going to do something different now?"
> The AA token utility is expanding to include settlement, payment, and collateral roles for AI agents — *on top of* its existing governance and money market roles. Nothing existing is being removed or downgraded.

### "Is my ISPO position safe?"
> Yes. The AA1→AA2 transition completed in November 2025 fully addressed any structural changes needed before this expansion. Your participation status is unchanged.

### "Will my Money Market collateral be impacted by agent-collateralization changes?"
> No. Agent-collateralization is an *additional* primitive being added to the Money Market over time. Existing collateral types, ratios, and positions are unaffected.

### "Are you going to dilute holders to fund the AI agent expansion?"
> No. The token supply remains fixed at 100M AA. No new emission, no holder dilution from this repositioning.

### "When's the TGE?"
> No date announced. We'll communicate it alongside the formal tokenomics publication. Community gets advance notice as always.

### "Is the team going to dump on us when AA lists?"
> Vesting schedules are designed for multi-year alignment and will be fully transparent in the tokenomics publication. Team unlocks track milestones, not calendar dates.

### "Should I do anything right now?"
> No action required. If you want to amplify the announcement, please do — but only if it resonates with you personally.

### "What's the best way to stay informed going forward?"
> Discord announcements channel (pinned), Telegram pins, this holder email list, and @AstarterDefiHub on X. AMA in 7 days will go deeper than any post can.
