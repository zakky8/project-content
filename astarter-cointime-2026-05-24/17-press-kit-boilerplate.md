More at astarter.io.*

### Long (≈200 words) — for media kits, sponsor pages, conference programs
*Astarter is a Cardano-native infrastructure platform building the settlement layer for the autonomous AI Agent economy. Originally launched in September 2021 as a joint venture backed by EMURGO — one of the three founding entities of the Cardano protocol — the platform has spent more than two years developing a four-product DeFi stack covering Launchpad and Launchpool issuance, a decentralized exchange, a collateralized money market, and a technical service platform for ecosystem builders. In 2025, Astarter formally repositioned that stack as the financial substrate for an emerging category at the intersection of decentralized finance and autonomous AI execution, known as DeFAI. The system is anchored economically by the AA token, capped at a fixed maximum supply of 100,000,000 units, with utility spanning agent-to-agent settlement, DePIN compute payments, protocol governance, and collateral for agent-issued credit. On May 15, 2026, Astarter announced its strategic alliance with social-growth network UXLINK, marking the first publicly coordinated stack to integrate AI Agent, DePIN, and DeFAI primitives under a single operational model. More at astarter.io | X: @AstarterDefiHub.*
