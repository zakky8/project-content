## Astarter and UXLINK Announce Strategic Alliance to Build Settlement Infrastructure for the Web 4.0 Autonomous AI Agent Economy

*Cardano-native DeFi platform partners with Web3 social-growth network to integrate AI Agent, DePIN, and DeFAI primitives under a unified stack*

---

**SINGAPORE / [Date Placeholder] — Astarter**, the Cardano-based decentralized finance infrastructure platform backed by EMURGO, today announced a strategic alliance with **UXLINK**, a leading Web3 social-growth network, to jointly develop the settlement infrastructure for the emerging Web 4.0 autonomous AI Agent economy.

The alliance combines Astarter's four-product DeFi stack — Launchpad, DEX, Money Market, and Tech Service Platform — with UXLINK's distribution surface across one of Web3's larger active social graphs, establishing the first publicly coordinated stack to integrate AI Agent execution, DePIN compute substrate, and DeFAI financial primitives under a single operational model.

### A Defining Infrastructure Gap

Industry research from Messari, a16z, and Delphi has converged on the AI Agent infrastructure category as one of the highest-conviction trades of the 2026 cycle, with the underlying market projected to grow from approximately $8 billion to over $50 billion within five years. The current generation of decentralized finance was built for human counterparties, lacking the deterministic settlement primitives that autonomous AI agents require to operate without human arbitration.

The emerging category addressing this gap — known as **DeFAI** — sits at the intersection of decentralized finance and autonomous AI execution. The Astarter/UXLINK alliance represents one of the first credible attempts to deliver an integrated stack covering all three foundational layers: the AI Agent itself, the DePIN substrate it runs on, and the DeFAI financial primitives it operates within.

### AA Token as Economic Anchor

The system is anchored economically by Astarter's native **AA token**, capped at a fixed maximum supply of 100 million units. AA functions across the stack as the unit of account for agent-to-agent settlement, the payment medium for DePIN compute resources, the governance instrument for protocol parameters, and a collateral primitive within the Money Market for agent-issued credit.

The platform completed its AA1-to-AA2 ISPO transition in November 2025, clearing the foundational tokenomics work required ahead of the eventual market launch.

### Forward Roadmap

Astarter has signaled three near-term execution priorities:

- Functional beta of the **DeFAI execution layer** within the coming quarters
- **Multi-chain expansion** beyond Cardano into EVM and Solana environments
- **Additional strategic alliances** of comparable scope to the UXLINK partnership

### About Astarter

Astarter is a Cardano-based DeFi infrastructure platform launched in 2021 as a joint venture backed by EMURGO, one of the founding entities of the Cardano protocol. The platform operates four production components — Launchpad, DEX, Money Market, and Tech Service Platform — and is repositioning its stack as the settlement substrate for the emerging Web 4.0 AI Agent economy.
