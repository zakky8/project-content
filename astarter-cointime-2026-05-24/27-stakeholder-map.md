# Stakeholder Map & Outreach Sequences

**This is the single highest-leverage document in the package.** Without named relationships, every other asset is broadcasting into a void. With them, every asset becomes a delivery payload to a specific receiver.

**Convention:**
- **Tier 1:** Pre-existing relationship OR can be warm-introduced in ≤2 hops
- **Tier 2:** Cold but addressable via known channel
- **Tier 3:** Cold, longer-game

**Outreach states:** `not contacted` → `pitched` → `discussing` → `committed` → `published` / `passed`

**All emails / handles flagged `[VERIFY]`** require GK or community team to confirm before sending.

## VI. Outreach Sequences (templates)

### Sequence A — Tier 1 Journalist (e.g., Sam Kessler at CoinDesk)

**Day 0 (warm intro path):**
> Find common connection in their Twitter following → ask connection for intro DM. Never cold-email Tier 1.

**Day 0 (cold, last resort) — Subject:**
> "DeFAI category piece — original analysis on agent settlement infra"

**Body:**
> Hi [Name],
>
> Quick note — I've been following your DeFi infrastructure coverage and think there's a piece you'd find interesting on the emerging settlement-layer problem in the AI agent economy.
>
> Original analysis attached: how Olas, Virtuals, Fetch.ai, and Astarter are each anchored at different points in the agent stack, and why the settlement layer is the slot none of them have fully claimed. Includes the May 15 Astarter × UXLINK alliance as a working case study.
>
> Available exclusively to one outlet under embargo if you want first-look. Otherwise broadly drop in 7 days.
>
> 5 minutes for a quick call this week?
>
> [Sign]

**Follow-up cadence:** Day +3, Day +7, then move on.

**Day 0 — DM:**
> Hey [Handle], saw your piece on [recent post]. We just dropped a thesis on settlement infrastructure for the agent economy that I think directly engages your framing. Mind if I send a 5-line summary + the deep-dive link if interested?

**If yes:** send concise summary + thought-leadership piece link.
**If RT:** engage their comments for 4 hours, amplify back.

## VII. CRM hygiene (the boring discipline that makes this work)

- Every outreach logged: date, channel, contact, message, response state
- Re-engagement timer: if no response in 5 days, follow up once; if no response in 10 days, move to next quarter
- Quarterly purge: anyone who hasn't engaged in 2 quarters gets dropped from Tier 1
- Win loop: any journalist / KOL who covers us gets a thank-you note within 24 hours and a personal heads-up on the next announcement before broad drop
- Never spam, never mass-CC, never use mailmerge for Tier 1
