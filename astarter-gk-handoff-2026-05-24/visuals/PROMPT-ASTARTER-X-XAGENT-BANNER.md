## 🎯 Why Option A is recommended

| Reason | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Visually different from prior banners** | Diagonal bicolor split is a complete departure from the isometric-cube + hex-grid Quiet Substrate template used for UXLINK / investors / roadmap. GK / Nicole / Fei-ge will see this is a NEW asset, not a recycled template. |
| **Both brands get equal weight** | Partnership banners often fail when one brand dominates. The diagonal split gives Astarter and X-Agent equal canvas real estate. |
| **Mint green + Violet = striking** | Complementary on the color wheel. High contrast = high recognition. Distinct from anything in either brand's solo materials. |
| **Tesla-arc center detail** | Adds the "moment of partnership" visual metaphor — the spark where two entities meet. Memorable. |
| **Clean negative space for logos** | AI tools cannot render brand logos accurately. By specifying negative space, the prompt ensures you composite the real logos in Figma without fighting AI hallucinations. |
